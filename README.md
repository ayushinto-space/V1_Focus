# V1 Focus: Aviation-Grade Productivity
V1 Focus is a focus management platform that treats your productivity sessions as official flight operations. By mapping Pomodoro-style deep work to real-world flight telemetry, V1 Focus transforms your task tracking into a professional pilot’s logbook, complete with mission archives, career progression, and live radar tracking.

## ✈️ Mission Philosophy
Productivity isn't just about timers; it’s about intent.  
1. Flight-Based Workflow: Every focus session is a "Flight." You have a departure (start), a cruise (deep work), and an arrival (completion).  
2. Operational Transparency: Track your performance via a "Blackbox" telemetry archive that calculates your On-Time Performance (OTP) and logs every mission status.

## 🚀 Key Flight Deck Features
1. Live Telemetry Radar: A fully interactive, responsive map interface that displays your mission trajectory in real-time, utilizing global aviation data APIs to visualize your focus "routes."  
2. Blackbox Analytics Dashboard: An automated, persistent logbook engine that tracks your career stats, including total flight hours, sector history and mission reliability percentages.  
3. Mission Accountability: An "Abort Mission" protocol that captures flight data even when a session is interrupted, providing an honest record of your focus stability.

## Getting Started
To begin your first operation, simply navigate to the terminal, enter your flight identifier (e.g., POMO for a standard deep-work block) and engage the Start sequence to initiate the countdown. Your performance will be logged to your internal Blackbox upon mission completion.
